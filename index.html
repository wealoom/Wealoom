Our Story

Woven to a Higher Standard

Wealoom is a manufacturer and exporter of premium carpets, crafted in Bhadohi — the region long regarded as the heart of India's hand-knotting tradition. Behind every piece are many years of craft, carried in the hands of the weavers we work with — never stamped out on a mass-production line.

We work in natural materials — knotting each carpet to order, in any size, colour, or design a space demands. For us, craftsmanship isn't a finishing touch. It's the entire point. A carpet should feel as considered as the room it's made for.

As a direct manufacturer and exporter, we ship to many countries — to hotels, interior designers, and homes — straight from our looms, without the layers of markup that usually sit between the weaver and the room.

In the Workshop

Behind the Loom

A glimpse into the hands and looms that bring every Wealoom carpet to life — unfiltered, unhurried, and entirely handmade.

Warping the Loom

Every carpet begins with thousands of warp threads, strung by hand before a single knot is tied.

Hand-Knotting

Our weavers tie each knot individually, building the design row by row over weeks or months.

Weft & Shuttle

Traditional wooden shuttles carry the weft yarn through the warp, locking the pattern into place.

Hand-Carving Detail

Skilled hands carve crisp geometric lines into the pile, giving each motif depth and definition.

Sculpting the Design

Multi-level trimming brings motifs like this to life, raising some areas and lowering others for texture.

Final Trim

A last pass with hand shears refines every edge, removing stray fibres for a clean, even finish.

Edge Finishing

The border is hand-stitched and trimmed to lock the weave and prevent fraying for years of use.

Binding by Hand

Two artisans work together to bind the edge of a rounded rug, a process unique to each shape.

Quality Check

Every batch of yarn is colour-matched against approved samples before it reaches the loom.

What is the difference between hand-knotted and hand-tufted carpets?

+

Hand-knotted carpets are woven entirely by hand — each knot tied individually onto the loom, making them the most durable and valuable. Hand-tufted carpets use a tufting gun to push yarn through a backing — faster to make but still handcrafted, and more affordable. Both are premium; hand-knotted is the pinnacle of the craft.

Can I order a custom size or colour?

+

Absolutely. Every Wealoom carpet can be crafted in a custom size, and most designs are available in alternate colour palettes. Share your room dimensions and preferred colours with us, and our team will guide you through the options and timeline.

How long does delivery take?

+

Ready-made carpets are typically delivered within 5–10 working days across India. Custom-made orders take 4–10 weeks depending on size, complexity, and technique. We'll keep you updated at every stage.

Is the Try at Home service really free?

+

Yes, completely free with no obligation to purchase. Our expert brings up to 3 carpet samples to your home, helps you see how they look in your space, and answers any questions. You only pay if you decide to keep a carpet.

How do I clean and maintain my Wealoom carpet?

+

Regular gentle vacuuming (suction-only, no rotating brush) 2–3 times a week is ideal. For spills, blot immediately with a clean white cloth — never rub. We recommend professional hand-washing every 2–3 years. See our full Carpet Care guide on this page.

Do you supply to hotels and commercial spaces?

+

Yes — hospitality and commercial projects are a core part of what we do. We offer bulk pricing, custom branding, and wall-to-wall installation for hotels, resorts, offices, and restaurants. Contact us for a tailored quote.

Carpet Care Guide

Preserve the Beauty

🧹

Regular Cleaning

Vacuum your carpet gently 2–3 times a week, always in the direction of the pile. Avoid rotating brush attachments on delicate hand-knotted pieces — use the suction-only mode.

💧

Spill Response

Act immediately. Blot — never rub — spills with a clean white cloth. For wool carpets, use lukewarm water with a drop of mild liquid detergent. Always blot from the outer edge inward.

☀️

Sunlight & Rotation

Rotate your carpet 180° every 6 months to ensure even wear and fading. Limit prolonged direct sunlight exposure, especially for silk and vibrant dye carpets.

🛡️

Padding & Protection

Always use a quality non-slip pad beneath area rugs. This prevents slipping, reduces pile wear, and helps the carpet maintain its shape and natural bounce.

🏭

Professional Washing

We recommend professional hand-washing every 2–3 years, or sooner for high-traffic areas. Our certified care partners will restore your carpet's original lustre.

📦

Storage

Roll — never fold — your carpet pile-side in, wrapped in acid-free paper or breathable cotton muslin. Store in a cool, dry, dark place away from moisture and pests.
